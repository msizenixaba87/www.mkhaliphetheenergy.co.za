About Us

Mkhaliphethe Energy Solutions is a South African-based manufacturer of renewable energy products. As a subsidiary of Mkhaliphethe Holdings, we focus on solar panel assembly, inverters, batteries, and custom solar kits designed to meet the energy demands of communities across Africa.

  • Inverters & Controllers
  • Battery Packs (Lithium/Gel)
  • Solar Kits for Homes & Schools
  • Portable Solar Lights
  • Solar Geysers
